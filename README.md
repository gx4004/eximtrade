## 📋 What is this?

This is the official website for **Eximtrade**, a Polish international trade & distribution company. It includes:

| Page | Description |
|------|-------------|
| 🏠 Homepage | Company overview & hero section |
| 🏢 About | Company background & team |
| ⚙️ Services | What we offer |
| 🏭 Industries | Product sectors we cover |
| 🗺️ Markets | Countries & regions we serve |
| 📬 Contact | Get in touch |
| 📄 Legal | Privacy, Cookies, Terms |

🌐 Available in **4 languages**: Polish 🇵🇱 · English 🇬🇧 · German 🇩🇪 · Russian 🇷🇺

---

## 🖥️ How to open the site locally

**Easiest way — no setup needed:**
1. Download this repository
2. Open the folder
3. Double-click `index.html` → opens in your browser ✅

**Proper local server:**
```
python3 -m http.server 8080
```
Then open **http://localhost:8080** in your browser.
